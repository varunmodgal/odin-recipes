Burger

Description

This is a cheese burger

Ingredients

  • burger buns
  • potato
  • cabbage
  • tomato
  • onion
  • butter

Steps

  1. Add butter to pan
  2. Cook burger buns at mild heat
  3. add cabage between burger buns
  4. add onion between burger buns
  5. add tomato between burger buns
  6. make patty out of potato
  7. add patty to burger
